Before You Buy

Consider these questions while you are still looking to buy an AI toy for your child.

  • Does your child need an AI toy or are traditional toys enough?
  • Can you easily find clear information about the toy and the company behind it?
  • Does the company list its team members; are any of them child psychology and safety experts?

Safe Use Tips

How to ensure safety if you have bought an AI toy.

  • Test the toy by yourself to learn how it reacts to possibly risky situations
  • Watch out for age-inappropriate answers and observe how your child responds to the toy
  • Treat AI-toy time like screen time: set limits and balance it with offline play, books, and human interaction

How to introduce AI toys
  • Start with an honest, age-appropriate explanation

  • Watch for signs of unhealthy attachment
  • Keep the conversation going as your child grows
